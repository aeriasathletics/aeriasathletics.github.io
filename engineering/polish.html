Vehicle Polisher

A final year product design creation, this was purely on SolidWorks, with a focus on the Product Design process.

Internal View of Polisher

Final Revision

Bevel gears were used to translate motion over 90 degrees, while multiple ball bearings distribute the load to the frame (and away from the important motor shaft). An ergonomic grip and lightweight frame contributed to the selection and detailed design of this product.

  • CAD/Solidworks
  • Concept Designs and Elimination
