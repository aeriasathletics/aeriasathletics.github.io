Yaw, Roll, Telescopic Bracket Hook

This project involved designing and building a custom bracket system with yaw, roll, and telescopic capabilities. The system is intended for modular applications requiring secure locking and precise motion control. It includes M8 and M12 locking bolts, rotational axis control, and easy telescopic adjustment for flexible installation.

The focus was on mechanical reliability, ease of assembly, and repeatable positioning. Stress and fatigue analyses were performed to ensure safe operation under varying load conditions.
