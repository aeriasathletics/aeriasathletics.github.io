Locating Pegboard

A pegboard used both for the calibration of a laser engraver and to hold metal base plates in their correct position for engraving.

Top view of pegboard, base plate, holding fixtures and simplified engraver.

Revision 4

The pegboard was designed with a pattern of circular holes that allow for the exact positioning of components for engraving. 3D Printed fixtures (using PLA) were manufactured to allow for easy location of the components on the board. Distances were calculated precisely for each variation of base plate.
