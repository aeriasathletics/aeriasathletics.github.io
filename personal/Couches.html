Home Project: Outdoor Couches

This project involved designing and building a set of durable, comfortable outdoor couches. The focus was on weather-resistant materials, modular assembly, and ergonomic comfort. I experimented with different construction techniques and finishes to create a functional and visually appealing seating area for outdoor use.

Key Contributions / Learnings

  • Designing for outdoor use and durability
  • Material selection: treated wood, hardware, and finishes
  • Assembly and joinery techniques for modular construction
  • Ergonomic seating design for comfort
  • Weatherproofing and finishing considerations
  • Documenting build process and iterations
