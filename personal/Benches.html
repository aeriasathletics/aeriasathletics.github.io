Home Project: Bench

This project involved designing and building a sturdy, comfortable bench for use around the home. The goal was to create something functional and aesthetically pleasing, while experimenting with woodworking techniques and joinery methods. I focused on durability, modularity, and clean design, producing a piece that is both practical and visually appealing.

Project Highlights

A home woodworking project

Key Contributions / Learnings

  • Designing for comfort, durability, and clean aesthetics
  • Material selection: wood types, finishes, and hardware
  • Cutting, assembly, and joinery techniques
  • Experimenting with modular and scalable components
  • Practical problem-solving and iteration during build
  • Documenting process and visual presentation
